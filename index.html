Our Work


Eyelash extensions patch test - $35

Patch tests are recommended to ensure there is no allergic reaction. Usually this service takes 30 minutes where I will put 10-15 lashes on each eye. The lashes used will match the natural lash in length and width so its not noticeable at all. The client should wait about 5-7 days to see if any allergic reactions occur. Really this service should be for clients who don't know if they are allergic to acrylic nails and want to be safe. It's ideal for people with big events like weddings. The last thing a bride wants is to be allergic to a full set she paid for and needs to be taken off because the wedding day is in a few days.

New client consultation (for clients with lash extensions on from a previous artist) - Free

This service takes 30 minutes. This service is not required but rather highly recommended. I take a look at their lashes to make sure all the extensions are isolated and not stuck together. If they are too stuck together, they will need to be removed and then a new set can happen. I won't let any clients walk out of the studio with lashes stuck together because it will cause permanent damage to the hair follicle.

This service takes two hours. Classic extensions is one extensions per one natural lash. It offers a darker, defined look. It is recommended for first time lash extension clients who want a mascaraed look. The fullness of classic sets will come naturally with the volume of your natural lashes.
